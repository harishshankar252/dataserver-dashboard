Objective of the project

In this project we triggered data uisng API's for every minute/second/hour/week from Environmental Sensors placed in 8 houses and visualized the data using Tableau and Power BI

Target audience

Specially Developed this dashboard for people. For all the people who works on Sensors, this will be an example to show your data interacively.
